About the Business

King's Men Home Security originally opened its doors in 2014 under the ownership of founder Larry O'Brien. A decorated policeman, he took offense to criminal activity within his home community of Belmont, Port-of-Spain. Leveraging his understanding of modern crime fighting techniques, years of experience training young recruits, and connections to prominent business owners, he devised a plan to safeguard Belmont residents.
After a successful debut, The company diversified itself in 2015 opening a storefront in the heart of Port-of-Spain focusing on providing citizens with powerful home Security systems and state of the art remote monitoring technology for affordable rates. His no nonsense approach to safety and excellent business accumen propelled the once small business to the regional giant it is now. Operating stores within Port-of-Spain, Trinidad, Scarborough, Tobago and most recently Bridgetown, Barbados.

Our Motto: Maximize customer satisfaction through customer protection.
Our Slogan: : You are the king of your castle, let the king’s men protect it.
